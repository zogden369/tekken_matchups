Dragunov Matchup

Dragunov is an rushdown character with many offensive and defensive tools. His goal is to stay close and steal frames with his many plus moves. He's also great at punishing mistakes.

  • WR2

    This is probably Dragunov's most famous move. A plus on block counter hit launcher that allows him to rushdown better than most of the cast. If blocked, don't press a button. Instead, try to predict when he'll use it and sidestep.

  • B1+2

    Another strong move for stealing frames. This move is plus on block and knocks you down on counter hit. Don't press anything if you block this.

  • DB3

    This move is a slow, high crushing, low launcher. Functions identical to Bryan's snake edge. Block it on reaction and launch it.

  • D2

    A high crushing poke that staggers on counter hit. Very useful for chipping at the opponent and fishing for counter hits. If blocked, punish with your 13 frame while standing move to make Dragunov think twice about just throwing this move out.

  • DF2

    Dragnov's best tool for whiff punishing and punishing unsafe moves. Don't let him abuse this and punish with your 12 frame if blocked.
