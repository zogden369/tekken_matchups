Marduk Matchup

Marduk is a grappler with many ways to mix you up. You generally want to pressure him so that he can't use any of his best tools.

  • Tackle

    Marduk's infamous tackle. If successful, it forces a three way mix up. It can be completely avoid by any lowcrushing move or by pressing 1+2 the instant you're grabbed. once you are grabbed you have to guess between 1,2, or 1+2. The numbers correspond to which hand he attacks with. 1=left, 2=right, 1+2=both.

  • B1+2

    This is Marduk's armor crush move. It's only -10 so you have to be sharp if you want to punish it on block. You could try anticipating the move and ducking, but Marduk can punish you heavily if he sees what you're doing.

  • DF1

    Marduk's best launcher in terms of speed and utility. Perfect for whiff punishing and anying -15 or more. It's only -13, so it's easy for Marduk to get away with throwing this move out. Make him think twice about using it by punishing every time.

  • QCF2

    This move is one of Marduk's best low options. It's unseeable and gives big damage on counter hit. Either sidestep left or punish with your 12 frame while standing move.

  • DB2

    One of Marduks best options in neutral. It's A safe, mid, counter hit launcher. It's only punishable on whiff. So either sidestep or backdash to force a whiff.
