Kazuya Matchup

Kazuya's main goal is to knock you down and mix you up. However, he is also able to punish his opponents mistakes heavily. He has a few very strong tools that help with this. Alot of his kit can be evaded by sidestepping left though. Take advantage of this.

  • CD4

    The best way to punish a hellsweep is to block it low and use your highest damage launcher. Blocking low too much againts kazuya is asking to get launched by another one of his moves further down this page. So essentially don't be predictable with your defense.

    Since you most likely won't know its coming so a safer option is to side step left. In fact, you can simply side walk left and you'll completely avoid the hellsweep. Kazuya does have some dangerous homing moves so be mindful and don't abuse this (unless of course your opponents refuses to adapt). Be aware that some combos don't work if you're not directly facing your opponent, so make sure to have a combo for this case.

  • CD2

    Arguablely the best move in the game, the ewgf(Electric Wind God Fist) is a +5 high launcher. If blocked, its still kazuya's turn. Meaning you should keep blocking unless you have a read. There are a few notable weaknesses however.

    Like most of kazuya's move you can simply sidestep left to avoid an ewgf. It cannot be sidewalked or sidesteped right.

    You can also duck and launch. Be careful doing this because you can be launched if kazuya catches on to what you're doing.

  • FF3

    This is kazuya's mid launcher. Its -3 so you can't punish it, but it is your turn so jab or something to stop kazuya's presure.

    If you want to punish, your best bet is to sidestep and launch. This move has no tracking.

  • DF12

    This move can lead to a guarenteed ff4 if connected so make sure to always punish with your 13 frame while standing move. Don't let kazuya get away with this.

  • 112

    The last hit of this string can be launch punished by most of the cast. However, its possible to hit confirm. Always be on the lookout to see if kazuya messes up the hitconfirm and punish him for it.
