Armor King Matchup

Armor King is grappler that excells at punishment. A lot of his moves give him plus frames but are balanced by being high. Take advantage of this weakness if you notice your opponent favors some moves.

  • F21

    This move is Armor King's main whiff punisher. The second hit is high so duck and punish if your opponent is abusing this move

  • CD1

    This move gives Armor King a whole 6 on block. If blocked, don't try to press anything because he has a fast counter hit launcher just for that. Your best bet is to duck premptively and punish.

  • CD2

    This is Armor King's other, more execution heavy whiff punisher. This move is also high, but the safer option is to wait and punish with your 10 frame.

  • DB3

    This move is a High crushing, low, counter hit launcher. Very good for catching people who jab too much. If blocked you can launch for a full combo. On normal hit it doesn't do much, so if you're patient you can bait Armor King into killing himself

  • F4

    This a mid move that gives plus frames. Don't try to press after blocking. It can be sidestepped to both sides however, so watch for patterns.
